Contributing to the Growth of
Local Texas Communities

We believe in being a strong community partner. We hope to share our gratitude and offer those in need a more promising future by giving back through charitable gifts, scholarship funds and food donations. By providing educational grants for the next generation of utility power workers and wind energy technicians, we’re also investing in tomorrow.

We’ve donated nearly $100,000 to fire stations, rodeos, food pantries and organizations such as the Medical League Center, the A.J. Swope Memorial Scholarship at West Texas A&M University, Amarillo College and Clarendon College.

“Cross Texas Transmission and its employees are very sincere about helping the communities they serve. The Medical Center League House in Amarillo has been very fortunate to be on the receiving end of their kindness. The past two years MCLH has received a grant, which has helped us help families who travel to Amarillo for medical care during their time of need, whether it is an emergency hospital stay, cancer treatments, hospice visits or funerals.”

Amber Glawe, President & Executive Director
Medical Center League House
