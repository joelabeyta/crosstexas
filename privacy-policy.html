Privacy Policy

Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, respects your right to privacy. This policy summarizes what personally identifiable information we may collect, and how we might use this information. This policy also describes other important topics relating to your privacy.

Information Collection

Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, will only collect personally identifiable information (including name, address, telephone number, fax number and/or e-mail address) that you voluntarily provide through our website or e-mail correspondence. We will collect general information (such as the type of browser you use, the files you request, the domain name and locality from which you request information) to improve our website and better meet your needs.

Use of Information

Participants are required to provide personal contact information so that Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, can utilize said information for public education and awareness.

The information you supply will be used by Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, to respond to your inquiries, supply you with requested information on Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, or track issues you report to Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, and crosstexas.com. Authorized third parties may be utilized by Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, to collect, track and process such information. If you have submitted personal information to Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, electronically and would like it removed or modified, please contact us at info@crosstexas.com. We will use reasonable efforts to comply with your request.

Children and Parents

Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, does not intend to collect personally identifiable information from children under age 18 without permission of the child's parent or legal guardian. Children under the age of 18 should submit personally identifiable information to Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, only with the explicit permission of their parent or legal guardian. Where appropriate, Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, will instruct children not to submit personal information. If your child has submitted personal information and you would like to request that such information be removed, you may do so as indicated above and Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, will use reasonable efforts to comply with your request.

Other Important Information

The Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, website may contain links to other sites. We do not endorse or otherwise accept responsibility for the content or privacy policies of those sites, unless held by Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, LS Power Associates, L.P., or its affiliates.

Crosstexas.com does not use "cookie" technology to remember your settings. The advantage to not using "cookie" technology is that we are not saving any information on your computer that might compromise your computer's security. You will not have to worry about accepting cookies - your personal computer's security remains intact.

It may be necessary, if required by law or if pertinent to judicial or governmental investigations, to release your personally identifiable information.

Cross Texas Transmission, LLC, reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. We will promptly reflect any such modifications in these Web pages.
